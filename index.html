About Us

Ravi Organic Fish is a trusted name in sustainable aquaculture, proudly operating across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. From the fertile waters of the Godavari basin to our inland farms near Warangal and Eluru, we raise fish in clean, chemical-free environments using eco-friendly methods.
